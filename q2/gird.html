Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of bangladesh

Rebel post

kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet,musician and philosopher of Bangladesh.Thef famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with thr revolution of that period of the British government.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh.He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the indian subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazru's writings explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity and revolution.He oppossed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including regilious,caste-based and gender-based.Throughout his career,Nazrul wrote short stories,novels and essays but is best known for his songs and poems.He poineered new music forms such as Bengali ghaals.Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records),collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
